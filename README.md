# Melodii
**Melodii** is a Nintendo-inspired audio player built for Android handhelds with dual displays. Designed with handheld devices like the [AYN Thor](https://www.ayntec.com/products/ayn-thor) in mind, Melodii separates functional aspects and informational aspects across two screens, similar to the UI philosophy used on systems like the Nintendo 3DS and Wii U.

###### Author Note
> "I started this project inspired by how familiar the [AYN Thor](https://www.ayntec.com/products/ayn-thor) felt to me as a 3DS owner and enjoyer. It made me want to make my experience even more similar to the original 3DS experience while still remaining modern and open. the [iiSU Frontend](https://iisu.network/) helped me a lot in achieving my goal, but as of now, it's just a nice UI. I want to bring back the experience of more specific software found on the 3DS such as Nintendo 3DS Sound and StreetPass. "

---

The bottom screen serves as a functional interface, providing playback controls, file navigation, and menus, while the top screen displays visual information such as album art, track details, and playback visuals.

---
**Completed Features**
- Dual-screen interface designed for multi-display Android handhelds

**Planned Features**
- Nintendo/iiSU-inspired UI
- Folder-based music library support
- Background playback with media controls
- Album art and metadata display
- Seek controls and playback management
- Controller / D-pad navigation support

**Potential Features**
- Launcher/Frontend integration ([iiSU](https://iisu.network/), [Cocoon]([iiSU Frontend](https://iisu.network/)), [ES-DE](https://es-de.org/)..)
- [StreetPass](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/SpotPass_and_StreetPass) alike features (Sharing music with passersby, Streaks)
- Audio effects and editing

---
###### Author Note
> "Some of these potential features such as Streetpass features could be seen in future projects of mine depending on if I decide to stick with this field of development."

---

Melodii is an attempt to recreate the whimsical, and tactile feel of classic Nintendo apps while making use of Android's flexibility.
